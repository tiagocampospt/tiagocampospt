What can I help you with?

Manuscript Writing

Regulatory Writing

Medical writing services that allow you to get the most of your clinical research data.

Manuscript Writing

  • Establishing a publication plan, including target journal identification and selection according to the authors requirements.
  • Writing the manuscript, according to the instructions for authors.
  • Submission to peer-review assessment, including support in responding to the reviewers’ and editor’s comments.
  • Final proof of accepted manuscripts, completing the publication process.

Editing

  • Editing manuscripts prior to submission to ensure they comply with international reporting standards and use proper English.
  • Editing manuscripts after submission, when the editor requests a native or bilingual revision. 
  • Editing academic theses and other scientific documents in the fields of medicine and clinical research.

Translation

  • Translating manuscripts originally written in Portuguese to English. In addition to the translation, the manuscript is also reviewed for its scientific content.
  • Translating PowerPoint presentations or posters to English, ensuring the proper use of the English language. 
  • Translating all types of scientific/technical documents in the fields of medicine and clinical research.

Regulatory Medical Writing

  • Planning clinical research projects, including the development of the study outline/protocol synopsis.
  • Writing documentation required for regulatory submission, including clinical study protocol, informed consent forms, and investigator brochure (if required). 
  • Writing the study report and manuscripts for publication, according to the publication plan established with the principal investigator.
