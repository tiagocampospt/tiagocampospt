ABOUT ME SERVICES

About Me

I'm a Medical Writer experienced both in medical communications and regulatory medical writing. Currently, I'm mostly focused on medical publications, particularly original research and review articles.

I have a strong academic background in clinical research and drug development, provided by the Master's in Pharmaceutical Medicine and the Bachelor's in Biomedical Sciences.

What can I help you with?

Manuscript Writing

I can help write your manuscripts for publication in international peer-reviewed journals.

Editing

I can help edit your manuscripts to make sure they comply with international reporting standards and use proper English.

Translation

I can help translate your manuscripts from Portuguese to English towards international submission and publication.

Regulatory writing

I can help prepare your regulatory submissions while planning interventional and observational research.
